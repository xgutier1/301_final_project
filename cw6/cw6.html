Tempe is a wonderful city that smells of rotting fish—and generally like death—for only a few months out of every decade! Here we are showcasing our great city that everyone flocks to from all over the world to study at the prestigious party school, Arizona State University. Explore the limitless opportunities for nightlife, education, and bad traffic that await you in The City of Tempe!

City of Tempe Attractions

The City of Tempe is home to the Tempe Town Lake and Tempe Town Lake Park where you can either take in the delicate aroma of decomposing fish or the previously notorious party school of Arizona State University where they actually do give you a decent education and can give you the tools you need to succeed on a beautiful campus that is being expanded practically on a quarterly schedule. Nightlife

The Tempe area actually has a solid nightlife that revolves mostly around Mill Ave, with spots like Varsity Tavern, Low Key Piano Bar, Whiskey Row, and so on. These places are great for 21+ students and those that want to break from the 9-5 grind on the weekends with some friends or for awkward tinder dates. Go check it out and get your drink on!

Arizona State University

ASU has 200+ 'prestigious faculty', whatever that means, 800+ National student scholars, 40+ elite programs, 270+ athletic championships, $4.3 billion in annual economic impact (for better or for worse), and 500+ thousand alumni. Whatever it is that you want to get out the area, the education is one to keep an eye on for a class to audit or for a full fledged degree that you've been wanting to go after for years.

Business in Tempe

Tempe is also a great place to work or conduct business at as it has multiple key industries that keep it alive and thriving as a city. The key industries are Advanced Business Services, Advanced Manufacturing, Healthcare and Biotechnology, General Technology, Tourism, and also has self-stated Outstanding employers if the other industries aren't your cup of tea.
