Argentina, Catamarca

The Good, The Bad, and The Rocky

This location has, by far, some of the roughest stages in the game and I'm sure that a lot of people would agree with me on this. But this is what makes it so much fun to race at. The tight corners and narrow roads provide and extra challenge with many don't cut calls and tons of places to shave off that extra second or two. The rocky sides of every stage at this location does, however, provide a great deal of danger to your front downforce, suspension, and tires. Soft tires are amazing on these stages, but they can very easily blowout if you miss a call putting you up against the rocky exterior of the narrow path you have to drive through.
