What I'm Setting Out To Do With DiRT Rally DB

This website as you can see is fairly barren. It's almost as barren as the field that I grow my fucks upon. I am no DiRT Rally mastermind, but some of you are. Or even if you aren't you still always have something interesting or important to share to the rest of the DR2 community and I...err...I mean WE want to hear it to get better and enjoy our game even more as the great gaming community we are. Please feel free to share any knowledge you have on this site as I try my best to keep it up to date. As you can already see it won't be perfect, but it is still better than nothing—a place for us to share our game knowledge and racing know how. So enjoy and be wary of the dreaded "don't cut"!
